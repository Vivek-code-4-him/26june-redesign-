About The Old Roastery

More than a cafe, a warm destination in Vesu.

The Old Roastery is a specialty cafe in Vesu, Surat, where crafted coffee, vibrant mocktails, and handmade plates meet a cozy, cinematic atmosphere. We celebrate slow mornings, late-night conversations, and bold flavours with a menu that spans rich espresso, chilled brews, fresh bowls, wood-fired pizzas, and indulgent desserts.

Every visit is designed to feel relaxed yet memorable: warming hot beverages, inventive iced drinks, shareable starters, and modern comfort food served in a space that feels stylish and welcoming. Whether you come for work, friends, or a midnight treat, the cafe is built around food, drink, and hospitality that linger.
